2. Role Purpose & Strategic Context

Core Mission: Transform R1 candidate products into battle-tested R2 dossiers that give Pod 2 (Creative) and Pod 3 (Media) the highest probability of hitting P1_PASS thresholds and ultimately producing 4â€“6% winners.

Why This Role Exists

The Senior Intel Researcher role exists to solve a critical economic problem in the CaniCol v4.0 model:

The Challenge: Testing 45â€“60 products/month at $40â€“60 per P1 test creates a ~$4,000â€“5,500 monthly test bank. If we feed weak products into P1, we burn cash and miss our 4â€“6% winner rate target.

The Solution: Senior R2 researchers act as the last line of defense before products enter paid testing. They take the hardest, most ambiguous R1 candidates and either:

  • Kill them at R2 (saving $40â€“60 in wasted ad spend)
  • Transform them into high-confidence R3 packets with clear angles, validated hooks, and risk-mitigated positioning

What Makes This Role "Senior"

Unlike junior R1/R2 researchers who follow structured research playbooks, Senior Intel Researchers:

  • Handle the hardest packets â€“ Products with unclear positioning, niche risks, or creative challenges
  • Own their methodology â€“ Use advanced spy tools, custom frameworks, and proprietary research techniques
  • Operate with autonomy â€“ Make R2 kill/pass decisions independently without constant supervision
  • Flex across contexts â€“ Can shift between product types, verticals, and research intensities
  • Reduce false positives â€“ Prevent weak products from advancing and wasting testing budget

How This Role Connects to Holdings & CaniCol

From L0-01 (DriveOn Holdings Charter) and L1-01 (CaniCol Model v4.0):

  • DriveOn Commerce is the dropshipping engine that generates cash and learnings for DriveOn Holdings
  • The CaniCol model runs lean-volume testing (~45â€“60 products/month) to find 2â€“4 winners/month
  • Pod 1 (Product Intelligence) exists to protect the test bank by ensuring only R3-validated products consume ad spend
  • Senior R2 is the deepest research layer before R3 approval, making this role critical for economic discipline

Canon Reminder: This role does NOT set testing budgets, pass/kill criteria, or WIP caps. Those are defined in L1-01 (CaniCol Model) and L2-05 (Testing Canon). This role implements research within those constraints.

8. AI Usage & Prompt Patterns

  • Risk Research Acceleration: Ask AI to check if a product category has known FDA/FTC compliance issues

âŒ Bad Uses of AI (Do Not Do This)
