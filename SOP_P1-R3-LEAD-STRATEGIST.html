1. Role Purpose & Context

Why This Role Exists: DriveOn's CaniCol v4.0 model runs 45-60 products/month through P1/P2 at $80-100 per product to find 2-4 winners monthly (4-6% hit rate). The R3 gate is the last research checkpoint before paid spend begins. Your strategic curation prevents false positives (weak products burning test budget) and optimizes for the right portfolio mix.

Mission Statement

Generate a predictable flow of 10-15 R3-approved packets per week (40-60/month) that:

  • Meet minimum P1 viability standards
  • Balance proven workhorse patterns with early trend opportunities
  • Maximize downstream P1_PASS rate through strategic selection
  • Feed Pod 2 (Creative) and Pod 3 (Media) with clear, actionable packets

Strategic Context

L0 Holdings: DriveOn Commerce operates as the lean-volume dropshipping engine within DriveOn Holdings, using systematic testing to validate products before brand investment.

L1 Model: CaniCol v4.0 emphasizes capital efficiency, kill discipline (70-80% die at P1), and portfolio thinking over individual product heroics.

L2 Pods: You bridge Pod 1 (research) with Pod 2 (creative) and Pod 3 (testing), ensuring only qualified products consume creative capacity and test budget.

4. Core Workflows

Why This Matters: These workflows are your decision engines. Follow them to maintain consistency and speed.
Workflow 1: R3 Packet Evaluation (Go/No-Go Decision)

Trigger

Researcher moves packet from R2_COMPLETE to R3_REVIEW in ClickUp and Notion

Inputs

  • Notion: Product Packets DB, packet ID PRD-YYYYMMDD-XXX, status = R3_REVIEW
  • ClickUp: Task in P1 ‚Üí Research Pipeline ‚Üí R3 Approved Packets list
  • Drive: Supplier docs, competitor screenshots in /Product Research/R2_Deep_Research/PRD-XXX/

Steps

  1. Open packet in Notion Product Packets DB
    • Scan sections: Offer Summary, Angles/Hooks, Supplier Options, Risk Assessment, Creative Notes
    • Check for completeness: all sections filled, no placeholders
  2. Evaluate Market Viability (5-10 min)
    • Review demand signals: are there active ads? TikTok videos? Social proof?
    • Check competitor performance data: CTR estimates, engagement
    • Ask: "Is there evidence people want this right now?"
  3. Evaluate Unit Economics (5-10 min)
    • AOV projection: is it ‚â•$35? (Below $35 rarely passes P1 economically)
    • Estimated CAC: based on niche CPCs, can we hit CAC ‚â§0.5√óAOV?
    • Margin check: supplier cost + shipping ‚â§ 30% of AOV?
  4. Evaluate Supplier Readiness (3-5 min)
    • 2+ suppliers listed with pricing and lead times?
    • Lead time ‚â§7 days? (Longer = P1 fulfillment risk)
    • Any supplier red flags? (bad reviews, MOQ issues)
  5. Evaluate Angle Clarity (5-10 min)
    • Are 2-3 distinct hooks/angles documented?
    • Are they specific enough for Pod 2 to create 4-6 creatives?
    • Do angles align with Creative Intel patterns?
  6. Risk Assessment (3-5 min)
    • Check for red flags: IP issues, compliance (FDA, FTC), logistics nightmares
    • If ANY red flag = auto-reject unless Tier S candidate
  7. Make Decision
    • GO (R3_APPROVED): Meets all criteria ‚Üí proceed to step 8
    • NO-GO (R3_REJECTED): Fails critical criteria ‚Üí proceed to step 9
    • REVISE (R2_REVISION): Fixable gaps ‚Üí proceed to step 10
  8. If GO ‚Üí Approve & Hand Off
    • Update Notion: Status = R3_APPROVED, Approved_By = Your Name, Approved_Date = Today
    • Update ClickUp: Move task to P2 ‚Üí Creative Pipeline ‚Üí Briefs list
    • Assign to P2-CR-DIRECTOR in ClickUp
    • Discord notification: Post in #p2-creative-briefs with packet ID and link
    • Add to your weekly R3 approval count tracker
  9. If NO-GO ‚Üí Document & Kill
    • Update Notion: Status = R3_REJECTED, Rejection_Reason = [specific reason]
    • Update ClickUp: Move to Closed/Killed status
    • Tag researcher in ClickUp comment: "@Researcher - Killed at R3. Reason: [X]. Focus on [Y] instead."
    • Log pattern in kill-reason tracker (for future research guidance)
  10. If REVISE ‚Üí Return to R2
    • Update Notion: Status = R2_REVISION, Revision_Notes = [specific gaps]
    • Update ClickUp: Move back to R2 Dossiers list
    • Tag researcher: "@Researcher - R3 revise. Missing: [X, Y]. Expected back in 24-48h."
    • Set reminder to follow up if not re-submitted in 48h

Quality Checks

  • Every decision has documented rationale (no silent approvals/kills)
  • Researcher gets actionable feedback within 24 hours
  • R3 approval rate stays 40-60% (too high = low bar, too low = researcher churn)

Typical Cycle Time

20-40 minutes per packet (experienced strategist). Aim for 3-5 packets per day = 15-25 per week.

Workflow 2: Early Trend Fast-Track

Trigger

You identify an emerging trend in TikTok/Minea/Creative Intel that needs fast R3 approval

Inputs

  • Trend signal source (TikTok Creative Center, Minea 7-day velocity, Creative Intel report)
  • Product idea with minimal R1 validation

Steps

  1. Validate Trend Signal (10 min)
    • Is this product showing 5-10x growth in last 7 days?
    • Are there 3+ successful ads in different accounts?
    • Does it align with DriveOn's brand positioning?
  2. Brief Researcher for Express R1‚ÜíR2 (same day)
    • Slack/Discord: "@Researcher - URGENT TREND: [Product]. Need R2 packet by EOD. Focus: supplier + 2 angles."
    • Set expectation: lightweight R2, just enough to pass R3 minimum bar
  3. Review Express Packet (end of day)
    • Apply R3 criteria but accept "good enough" on depth
    • Priority: speed to P1 test before trend cools
  4. If Approved ‚Üí Flag as EARLY_TREND
    • Add "EARLY_TREND" tag in ClickUp and Notion
    • Hand off to Pod 2 with note: "Trend product - prioritize creative by [date]"
    • Track separately: early trend bets should be 3-5/week minimum

Quality Checks

  • Early trend bets are 3-5 per week (not 0, not 10)
  • They still meet R3 minimum bar (not random hype products)
  • Track their P1_PASS rate separately to validate strategy
Workflow 3: Weekly Portfolio Mix Review

Trigger

Every Friday afternoon, review this week's R3 approvals for portfolio balance

Inputs

  • Notion Product Packets DB filtered by R3_APPROVED, This Week
  • ClickUp R3 Approved Packets list
  • Weekly throughput dashboard

Steps

  1. Count This Week's R3 Approvals
    • Target: 10-15 approvals
    • If below 10 ‚Üí identify bottleneck (researcher capacity? quality issues?)
    • If above 15 ‚Üí check if quality bar slipped (too easy to pass?)
  2. Categorize by Risk Profile
    • Proven Patterns: Products similar to past P1_PASS winners
    • Trend Bets: Early trend products
    • Experimental: New angles, untested niches
    • Target mix: 60% proven, 30% trend, 10% experimental
  3. Check Niche Diversity
    • Are we over-indexed on one niche? (e.g., all beauty, no home goods)
    • Flag for next week: "Need more [niche] candidates"
  4. Review P1 Feedback Loop
    • Check last 2 weeks' P1 results: which R3 packets are passing P1?
    • Identify patterns: what's working? What's dying at P1?
    • Adjust next week's R3 selection criteria based on P1 data
  5. Update Researchers on Priorities
    • Slack #p1-research-updates: "Next week focus: [niche/pattern], reduce [overindexed area]"
    • Share P1 learnings: "Last week's wins were [X pattern], let's find more"

Quality Checks

  • Weekly R3 approval count is 10-15 (feeds 40-60/month target)
  • Portfolio mix is balanced (not all safe, not all risky)
  • P1_PASS rate is tracked and improving (target: 20-30% of R3 approvals pass P1)
