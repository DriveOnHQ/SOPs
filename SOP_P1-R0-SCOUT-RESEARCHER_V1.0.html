üéØ Your Mission: Feed the Pipeline with High-Potential Product Signals

As an R0 Scout, you are the first filter in DriveOn's product testing machine. Your job is to scan the digital landscape at high speed, capture promising product signals, and pass qualified ideas to R1 researchers. You focus on breadth over depth ‚Äî finding 20-30 ideas weekly, with 8-12 advancing to deeper research.

  • 8-12 ideas advanced to R1 stage
  • Pipeline stays full and flowing
  • Obvious bad fits filtered early

You Own

  • Platform scanning & trend monitoring
  • Quick plausibility filtering
  • R0 ‚Üí R1 handoff decisions

WIP & Rhythm

  • WIP Cap: 12 R0 ideas in progress
  • Cycle time: 1-3 hours per idea
  • Weekly throughput: 20-30 scans

‚öñÔ∏è
CaniCol Model Economics (L1-01, L2-05)

Your scouting feeds a system targeting 45-60 products/month reaching P1/P2 tests. To hit this, Pod 1 needs 12-15 R3 packets weekly. Your R0 ‚Üí R1 conversion rate (roughly 40-60%) must maintain pipeline velocity without flooding downstream researchers.

‚ö†Ô∏è
WIP Discipline (L2-03)

Never exceed 12 R0 ideas in active scouting at any time. When you hit WIP cap, you must either kill weak candidates or hand off qualified ones to R1 before capturing new signals. WIP violations trigger capacity reviews, not WIP inflation.

Core Workflows

Step-by-step execution guides for your main responsibilities

üîç Platform Scanning & Signal Capture

Trigger: Daily scheduled scanning blocks Frequency: 2x daily (morning + afternoon) Duration: 90-120 min per block

üì• Inputs

  • Your curated feed sources (TikTok FYP, Meta Ad Library, Minea filters)
  • Current WIP count from ClickUp R0 Ideas list
  • Strategic themes from Pod Lead (if any special focus this week)

‚öôÔ∏è Steps

  1. Check WIP headroom: If at 10-12 active R0 ideas, kill 2-3 weak ones before scanning. Never scan if at WIP cap.
  2. TikTok scan (30-45 min):
    • Scroll FYP and "For You" ad feed for 15-20 min ‚Äî look for repetition (same product, multiple creators)
    • TikTok Creative Center ‚Üí Top Ads ‚Üí Filter by Views/Engagement spikes in last 7 days
    • Check trending sounds/hashtags ‚Äî any product-related themes emerging?
    • Note 3-5 product signals with basic metadata (product type, hook angle, engagement pattern)
  3. Meta Ad Library scan (30-45 min):
    • Search broad categories (beauty, fitness, tech, home) + "Shop Now" / "Learn More" filter
    • Sort by "Recently Active" ‚Äî look for ads that just launched (1-7 days old) with high spend indicators
    • Click through to landing pages ‚Äî note product, offer, pricing, angle
    • Cross-reference with Minea to see if it's a known winner or new launch
  4. Minea spy tool session (30-45 min):
    • Filter: Last 7 days, Engagement spike, US/UK/CA markets
    • Scan "New Winning Products" feed ‚Äî look for 5k+ engagement, 100k+ views
    • Check supplier links ‚Äî is it available on AliExpress/Alibaba? MOQ reasonable?
    • Note 3-5 candidates that pass initial sniff test
  5. Quick AliExpress/Alibaba check (20-30 min):
    • For top 5-8 signals from above, verify supplier availability
    • Check: orders (500+?), reviews (4.5+ stars?), shipping options (to US/UK?)
    • Red flag: MOQ >100 units, long lead times (>30 days), quality complaints
    • Pass: Reasonable pricing, multiple suppliers, fast shipping options
  6. Log signals in ClickUp (10-15 min):
    • For each qualified signal, create R0 task in ClickUp ‚Üí P1 Product Intelligence ‚Üí Research Pipeline ‚Üí R0 Ideas
    • Task name: PRD-YYYYMMDD-XXX ¬∑ [Product Name]
    • Fill custom fields: Product_ID (generate), Pod (P1), Gate (R0), Status (Backlog ‚Üí Active)
    • Task description (brief bullet format):
      • Signal source (TikTok/Meta/Minea/Other)
      • Product type & core promise
      • Hook/angle observed
      • Supplier link (if found)
      • Initial gut: "Strong signal" / "Maybe" / "Weak but curious"
  7. Update Notion Product OS: For top 3-5 signals, create placeholder pages in Notion Product OS with status "R0_Scouting" linked to ClickUp task.

üì§ Outputs

  • 8-12 new R0 tasks created in ClickUp per scan block
  • 3-5 Notion Product OS placeholder pages for strongest signals
  • Updated WIP count (should not exceed 12 total R0 active)
‚ö†Ô∏è
Common Failure Mode: WIP Bloat

Don't scan if you're at WIP cap. Don't capture "maybes" just to fill quota. Quality > quantity. If you're consistently hitting 20+ captures but only 4-5 advance to R1, you're being too lenient at R0. Tighten your filter.

‚úÖ R0 Plausibility Check & Kill/Advance Decision

Trigger: After capturing signal, before handoff Frequency: Daily, end-of-day Duration: 5-10 min per product

üì• Inputs

  • R0 tasks in ClickUp with status "Active" (captured during scanning)
  • Basic product info from task description (signal source, product type, hook, supplier link)

‚öôÔ∏è Steps (5-10 min per product)

  1. Run R0 Kill Checklist (2-3 min) ‚Äî if ANY of these are true, kill immediately:
    • ‚ùå Obvious scam/pyramid/MLM pattern (health claims, income promises, "system")
    • ‚ùå Platform policy violation (weapons, adult, drugs, counterfeit brands)
    • ‚ùå Impossible logistics (perishable, oversized, requires special licensing)
    • ‚ùå Zero demand signal (no trend, no ad activity, no social proof)
    • ‚ùå Saturated commodity with no unique angle (generic phone case, basic tee, etc.)
    • ‚ùå No viable supplier (only 1-2 suppliers, MOQ >100, lead time >45 days)
  2. Check for "Pass to R1" signals (2-3 min) ‚Äî need at LEAST 2-3 of these:
    • ‚úÖ Clear trend spike (multiple creators, rising engagement, recent surge)
    • ‚úÖ Strong hook/angle visible in creative (problem/solution, transformation, curiosity gap)
    • ‚úÖ Reasonable economics (estimated landed cost allows 2-3x markup, AOV $30-150 range)
    • ‚úÖ Multiple suppliers available (3+ on Ali, reasonable MOQ, fast shipping)
    • ‚úÖ Engagement quality (comments asking where to buy, saves, shares)
    • ‚úÖ Competitor validation (similar products getting traction, not oversaturated yet)
  3. Make decision (1 min):
    • KILL (R0_FAIL): If failed kill checklist OR fewer than 2 pass signals ‚Üí Move to "Killed" status in ClickUp, add 1-2 sentence kill reason in comments
    • ADVANCE TO R1: If passed kill checklist AND has 2-3+ pass signals ‚Üí Update status to "R1_Shortlist", assign to R1 researcher (if known), add handoff note
    • PARK (rare): If borderline but interesting ‚Üí Tag as "R0_Parked", move to separate ClickUp list for later review
  4. Update Notion Product OS: For advanced products, update status from "R0_Scouting" to "R1_Shortlist". For killed products, archive or mark as "R0_Killed".

üì§ Outputs

  • 8-12 R0 ‚Üí R1 handoff decisions made daily
  • Clear kill log (1-2 sentence reason per killed product)
  • Clean R1_Shortlist queue for downstream researchers
  • WIP back under cap (target 8-10 active R0 at EOD)
üí°
Speed vs Accuracy Trade-off

R0 plausibility is intentionally shallow. You're not doing deep research ‚Äî that's R1/R2's job. Aim for 70-80% accuracy on pass/fail decisions. If you're spending >10 min per product at R0, you're going too deep. Trust your gut, document your reasoning in 1-2 sentences, and move on.
